The "AI Tax" Effect

HBM Dominance

Manufacturers (Samsung, SK Hynix, Micron) are shifting wafer capacity to High Bandwidth Memory (HBM) for AI GPUs like NVIDIA's H200/B200, starving the consumer market of standard DDR5.

Enterprise Migration

AI data centers are consuming high-capacity NAND at unprecedented rates. Enterprise SSD prices rose 200%+ in 2025, pulling consumer prices up in their wake.

Supply Discipline

Unlike previous boom-bust cycles, fabs are maintaining low production volume to protect profit margins, ensuring prices stay high through late 2026.

Pro-Tip: When to Buy?

Market analysts suggest the Peak will occur in H1 2026. A stabilization is expected by H2 2026, but meaningful price drops likely won't appear until 2027 as new manufacturing capacity comes online.
